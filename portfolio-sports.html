Sports & Entertainment

From championship golf communities and luxury sports labs to professional soccer franchises — Vimtra's sports and entertainment portfolio spans two continents, building high-value destinations and enduring fan-centric brands.

Golf Communities

World-Class Golf

Frisco, Texas · USA

The Bays — Golf Lab & Suites

A luxury, master-planned golf lab and suites development in Frisco, Texas — offering championship golf, upscale amenities, and exceptional residential living in one of North America's fastest-growing markets.

Near Mahabalipuram · India

Arka Grand

A landmark golf community development near Mahabalipuram on the Chennai–Pondicherry corridor — blending world-class golf with premium residential offerings in one of India's most scenic coastal settings.

India · Premier League Golf

IGPL — Indian Golf Premier League

A visionary initiative bringing premier league-format golf competition to India — creating a new spectator sport and driving golf participation across the subcontinent through structured team-based competition.

Soccer Franchise

Dallas Sidekicks

Dallas–Fort Worth · Texas

Dallas Sidekicks

One of the most storied indoor soccer franchises in America, the Dallas Sidekicks are a professional soccer property with deep roots in the Dallas–Fort Worth community. Vimtra's ownership focuses on building long-term value through fan engagement, community programming, and strategic franchise development. The Sidekicks represent Vimtra's commitment to sports as a catalyst for community connection and enduring brand equity.
