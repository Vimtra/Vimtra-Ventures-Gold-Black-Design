Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

Vimtra Ventures ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with our website at vimtra.com. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard information you provide to us.

Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • The subject and content of your message
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited (via standard server logs)

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial account details, government ID numbers, or health data through this website.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and correspondence
  • Communicate about partnership or investment opportunities you have initiated
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our website loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may collect usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. No other third-party analytics or tracking services are embedded on this site.

Data Retention

We retain contact information only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for any ongoing business relationship that may follow. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us directly.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect information submitted through this website. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the address below.
