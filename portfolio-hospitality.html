Featured Property

Kotagiri, Nilgiris · Tamil Nadu, India

The Windgrove

Nestled in the cool heights of Kotagiri in the Nilgiris, Tamil Nadu, The Windgrove is a premium destination retreat that harmonises with the breathtaking natural landscape of the Ooty region. Designed for discerning travellers seeking an escape into nature without compromising on luxury, The Windgrove sets a new benchmark for hill-station hospitality in South India — combining thoughtful architecture, curated interiors, and an immersive experience of the Nilgiri hills.
